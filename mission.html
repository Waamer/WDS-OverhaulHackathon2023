The Mission

Stephanie Wong's mission with InkLink is inspired by her journey as an immigrant student in Canada. As a tech-savvy entrepreneur, she founded InkLink to revolutionize learning accessibility. The app, more than a note-taking tool, is committed to inclusivity, addressing the needs of students facing language barriers and disabilities. Through features like speech-to-text, screen readers, and language translations, InkLink aims to create an inclusive learning environment. Stephanie envisions a warm and inviting website with a simple design, prioritizing accessibility considerations for all users.
